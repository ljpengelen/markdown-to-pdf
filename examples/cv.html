Profile

Luc is a software developer that combines a solid theoretical foundation in computer science with practical experience in developing web and mobile applications. After obtaining his PhD at the Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), he worked as a Postdoctoral researcher and teacher at the same university before switching to industry. As a software developer, he worked on Java, Rails, and Python back ends for a variety of web applications, including a number of self-service portals for customers of credit card resellers and an enterprise service bus. Additionally, he has worked on an SDK for iOS, native iOS applications, cross-platform mobile applications, and as a full stack developer on a number of Ruby on Rails applications.

Within Signify's indoor navigation department, Luc created a web application for the creation of mapping data.
